Planner

CPU scheduler simulator

An educational software that simulated scheduling algorithms for the processor on simple to complex examples, with strategies such as shortest job first and round robin, with preemption and other flavors.

The software was developed by Vlad Manea*, Codrin Dițu and Codruț Burdujoc when they were in college at the University of Iasi.

The application was submitted in partial fulfillment of the course on operating systems at the Faculty of computer science, University of Iasi. All students obtained the maximum grade for the course.
