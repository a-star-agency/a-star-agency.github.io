.campion

educational archive

A web application that organized the 1500 algorithmic tasks in 10 years of the .campion performance learning program, made easy to find and linked to original articles and educational software lessons.

The .campion educational archive was implemented by Vlad ManeA* and the two professors who managed the .campion program: Emanuela Cerchez and Marinel Șerban, also the ones who first taught Vlad how to code.

The archive launched in September 2009, after 9 months of development. Since then the archive has been used by students and teachers across the country. To this day, the grader receives solutions every minute.

A presentation paper and showcase of the .campion educational archive obtained the excellence award at the educational software section of the 7th edition of the National Conference on Virtual Learning in 2009.
