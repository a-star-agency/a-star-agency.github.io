DP

educational software

Explained three classic dynamic programming problems on examples, step by step: the discrete knapsack, the longest increasing subsequence and the triangle maximum sum. Fun stuff, if you are into algorithms. :)

The software was developed by Vlad Manea*, Vlad Tudose* and Ioana Marțin, coordinated by professors Emanuela Cerchez and Marinel Șerban, when Vlad and Vlad were interested specifically in algorithms.

The software obtained a mention award at the county phase, educational software section of the InfoEducation in 2006. This was the first complete software product developed by our Vlads.
