KMP

educational software

An educational software on the Knuth Morris Pratt (KMP) algorithm that simulated and explained it step by step on carefully selected examples, as well as evaluated the understanding of the algorithm.

The software was developed by Vlad Manea*, Vlad Tudose* and Andrei Albeșteanu, coordinated by professors Emanuela Cerchez and Marinel Șerban, when the Vlads were learning algorithms.

Implemented in 3 months, it won the Siveco Cup national educational software contest in 2008. The software was showcased at, and the team participated to the global education forum Online Educa Berlin.

An improved version of the software was acquired by Siveco Romania and integrated into the national Advanced e-Learning system (AEL), where most if not all schools in the country have access.
