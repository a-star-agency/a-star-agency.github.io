Jazzup

culture & art magazine

A collection of articles on books, movies, painters etc. written by students from the Faculties of computer science and mathematics. Served as fertile field for experiments on various web technologies.

The Jazzup culture & art magazine was founded by four students from the departments of mathematics and computer science: Flavia Anița, Andrada Aștefănoaie, Codrin Dițu and Vlad Manea.

The first version was submitted in partial fulfillment of the course on communication at the Faculty of computer science, University of Iasi. All students obtained the maximum grade.
