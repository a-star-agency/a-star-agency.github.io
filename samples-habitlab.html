Habitlab

habit formation iOS application

An iOS app to help people build good habits. Featured a menu with scheduling, programs, progress tracking and social activities, for running, reading, unplugging and reconnecting with friends.

Christina Lauer and Vlad Manea* were among the co-founders of the Habitlab startup in Copenhagen, Denmark and led an engineering and design team of 5 people each. See the complete team who contributed to the app here.

The implementation started in a November 2015. The first App Store release was in March 2016. Until July 2016 the team improved the app by releasing new features at a cadence close to the Apple review time: 3-5 days.

Habitlab qualified in 2016 to the Thinkubator think-tank and incubator, a program for high-potential startups with an acceptance rate of 7% in Copenhagen. We worked for 4 months alongside other 9 startups.
