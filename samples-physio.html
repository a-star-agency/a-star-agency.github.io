physio

physiotherapy iOS application

Habitlab's first specialized iOS application for physiotherapists to increase compliance in their patients through 1:1 personalized programs. Developed with the participation from a physiotherapist.

Christina Lauer and Vlad Manea* were among the co-founders of the Habitlab startup in Copenhagen, Denmark and led an engineering and design team of 5 people each. See the complete team who contributed to the app here.

Habitlab implemented this app in only 2 months, while at the Thinkubator think-tank and incubator hosted at a repair shop garage turned into a vibrant and entrepreneurial working space, part of Dare2Mansion.
