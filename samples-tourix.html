TouriX

travel web application

A travel planner that allowed users to schedule their flights, hotels, events and car rentals, as well as add landmark checkpoints on a map. It used a variety of web services from Google, Picasa, Kayak and Panoramio.

The travel web application was implemented by Vlad Manea* and Vlad Tudose*, along with two other friends, Codrin Dițu and Codruț Burdujoc, when they were college students at the University of Iasi.

The application implemented in 3 months was submitted to the course on web technologies at the Faculty of computer science, University of Iasi. All of us obtained the maximum grade and bonus.
