Counseling

educational web portal

A web application with resources for the counselling classes, containing original content organized in modules and submodules, as well as educational software, planning, questionnaires, presentations and more.

The software was developed by Vlad Manea*, Alexandru Periețanu and Vlad Constantinescu, managed by professors Claudia Cărăușu - scientific coordinator and Emanuela Cerchez - technical coordinator.

The counselling class educational portal was implemented in 5 months and qualified to the final round of the Siveco Cup national educational software contest, where it ranked on the fourth place.

A paper on the portal obtained the Intel Education excellence award at the 6th edition of the National Conference on Virtual Learning.
