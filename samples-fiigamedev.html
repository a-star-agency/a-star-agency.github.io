FII Gamedev

Gamedev club website

Brand identity and website for the game development club organized at the Faculty of computer science, University of Iasi. FII Gamedev was the first game development club in a Romanian university.

The FII Gamedev club was co-founded by Adrian Popovici and Vlad Manea* while they were students at the University of Iasi. The club was affiliated to the local branch of Microsoft Student Partners for one year.
